    Simple shape test¶

    Purpose¶

    Visualize the activity of internal mini-columns inside a BCPNN network while 2 trained patterns competing with each other.

    Metholoty¶

    The tested BCPNN network has 100 HCUs to represent a 10x10 image. Each HCU has 10 MCU to represent 10 different possible colors of each pixel. Connectivity rate is set to be 50%.

    Structural plasticity:

    • Grow connections at every 100 ms.
    • Never remove connections.

    Adaptation: Disabled

    Training¶

    Pattern No. Description
    1 Squre in north west corner
    2 Squre in north east corner
    3 Squre in south west corner
    4 Squre in south east corner
    5 Cross
    6 Random dots with 25% probability
    7 Random dots with 25% probability
    8 Random dots with 25% probability
    9 Random dots with 25% probability
    10 Random dots with 25% probability

    Test¶

    Pattern No. Winner
    1 vs 6 6
    2 vs 7 2
    3 vs 8 3
    4 vs 9 4
    5 vs 10 5
